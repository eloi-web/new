Latest Venues

Mezpark Gardens

Mezpark Gardens, located on KG 22 Avenue in Kinyinya, Kigali, is a picturesque event venue ideal for capturing memorable photographs.

Hôtel Des Mille Collines

Hôtel Des Mille Collines is the perfect venue for hosting unforgettable weddings and meeting all your event needs.

Centre Saint Paul

Centre Saint Paul is a top wedding venue in Kigali, featuring a lush green garden that provides a stunning setting for your special day.

Soleil Garden

Soleil Garden, located on KK35 Avenue in Kigali, offers a picturesque and affordable wedding venue ideal for couples seeking beauty on a budget.

Crown Conference Hall Kigali

Crown Conference Hall Kigali is a popular venue known for hosting weddings and large events, offering top-notch, budget-friendly services tailored to your needs.

Kigali Marriott Hotel

Kigali Marriott Hotel is a 5-star venue offering multiple wedding spaces, including the Kilimanjaro Ballroom for up to 200 guests and two scenic garden venues, Malaika and Ituze.

T-Park – Center

T-Park Center is a rising star among Kigali’s wedding venues, offering a unique blend of contemporary style and authentic charm for unforgettable celebrations.

Radisson Blu Hotel & Convention Centre, Kigali

Radisson Blu Hotel & Convention Centre, Kigali, is a top wedding venue hosting up to 2,600 guests with full wedding services including planning, catering, and decoration.

RPinnacle Gardens Kigali

Pinnacle Gardens, featuring beautiful gardens, an open-air pavilion, and a sparkling waterfall, offers a personalized setting for your dream wedding.

Kigali Serena Hotel

Kigali Serena Hotel takes your wedding personally by making it a celebration you will remember for the rest of your life. They offer both small and large venue spaces, depending on the number of guests you have.
